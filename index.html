What We Do

Real Robotics Projects

Work with drones, autonomous vehicles, humanoid robots, robot dogs, sensors, actuators, and embedded systems.

AI and Software

Explore perception, computer vision, SLAM, robot simulation, AI algorithms, and practical programming workflows.

Training and Mentorship

Join workshops, tutorials, internal projects, and peer learning paths designed for beginners and advanced builders.

Growth Path

Tier 3 Build foundations, learn tools, and join workshops.
Tier 2 Take on club projects and grow across multiple technical areas.
Tier 1 Lead internal projects, compete, mentor teammates, and earn recognition.
Competitions Prepare for RoboMaster, RAiCON, RoboCup Singapore Open, and more.

Build Intelligence. Create Impact.

The club welcomes FENG students interested in mechanical design, electronics and control, perception and AI vision, software, and robotics competitions. Beginners can start with guided training, while experienced members can join core teams and lead hands-on projects.
